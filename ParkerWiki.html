Parker Hahn is truly the goat. He has done a lot in his life and is constantly trying to make new memories. He always attempts to make the most out of any situation. Parker's favorite sport is Soccer and still plays pick up games with friends. He also loves to watch Soccer, with his favorite teams being FC Barcelona, Arsenal, and The Philadelphia Union, as well as having a soft spot for Leeds United. His all-time favorite Soccer player is Lionel Messi. Parker's favorite video games are FIFA 23, Rocket League, Mario Kart, Super Smash Bros, and Pokemon Scarlet. His current favorite board game to play is Wingspan, but he also really enjoys playing Exploding Kittens, Century, Munchkin, and Mastermind. Parker is also still an avid Wordle player.

Here are some fun facts about Parker. He has played at least a season of all the following sports; Soccer, Baseball, Tennis, Lacrosse, Bowling, and Rugby. In 2021, Parker went to Spikeball Nationals with one of his friends, and they got 15th in the Advanced bracket. Parker was a camp counselor his junior and senior year of high school, going with 6th graders to Camp Swatara. Parker has two dogs named Peanut and Hazel(nut). Parker also has a cockatiel named Francis who is older than his brother. As a senior in High School, Parker went on a Marine Biology trip to Jamaica. Parker's go-to break food when working at Chick Fil A is a spicy chicken sandwich with american cheese, lettuce, pickles, and bacon with a large fry and Chick Fil A sauce.


Early life

Parker Hahn was born in Reading, Pennsylvania on July 8th, 2001. Parker was the first son of parents Tammy and Richard Hahn. The person writing this wikipedia page does not know much about Parker's life when he was really young. On April 6th, 2005, Parker's family welcomed another boy, named Spencer, making Parker an older brother. Parker attended elementary school at Spring Ridge Elementary School before being redistricted to Whitfield Elementary School before fifth grade. However, because of the redistricting, Parker met three of his closest friends that he still is friends with to this day. Parker attended middle school at Wilson West Middle School. His mom was his 6th grade math teacher at Wilson West. In middle school, Parker played soccer, and did track and field, throwing shot put and discus, as well as running the 200m and 4x4 relay. Parker went to Wilson High School from 2016 to 2020, making many memories throughout those years. In high school, Parker had exceptional grades, and was inducted into National Honors Society as a junior. Parker played soccer in his freshman year, scoring 11 goals but stopped playing soccer that year. As a junior, Parker started dating his current girlfriend and also created the Wilson High School Spikeball Club along with some of his friends. They regularly played Spikeball with each other and newer members of the club. Unfortunately, as the club started to gain traction during his senior year, Covid-19 happened and Spikeball Club could not run. Parker graduated in May of 2020.

College Experience

After graduating in 2020, Parker committed to Penn State Berks, majoring in Computer Science, and planned on doing thr 2+2 program. During his sophomore year, Parker changed majors to IST, focusing on application and development. This change of major also meant he could stay at Penn State Berks and complete his four-year degree there. In the spring semester of sophomore year, Parker joined the Penn State Berks Rugby Team. In his first season of 7's, he scored one try, and his team went to playoffs in Gettysburg, before falling short to Widener. In the fall of his junior year, Parker played his first season of 15's. The Berks Rugby Team went 5-0 in the regular season, meaning the won the division and got to host a playoff game, but losing to Widener once again. Parker's fall semester of junior year was his best academic semester as well, achieving his first 4.0 of his college career. He currently has a 3.7 GPA overall.
